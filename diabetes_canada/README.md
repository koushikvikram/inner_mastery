## Key Concepts

- [Handy Portion Guide and Plate Method](https://www.google.com/search?q=diabetes+canada+handy+portion+guide+plate+method) — both hands for vegetables, fist for grains, palm for protein, thumb tip for fats; half plate vegetables, quarter protein, quarter grains
- [Available carbohydrates — fiber subtraction](https://www.google.com/search?q=available+carbohydrates+total+carbs+minus+fiber+diabetes) — Total Carbs minus Fiber = what actually raises blood sugar; lentils, chickpeas, and whole wheat chapati buffer spikes
- [Strategic South Asian dining swaps](https://www.google.com/search?q=diabetes+south+asian+dining+out+papad+chapati+portion) — papad over samosa, chapati over paratha, raita over creamy dressing, fruit over mithai — honor heritage without surrendering targets
- [Glucose targets and meal spacing](https://www.google.com/search?q=diabetes+blood+glucose+target+4-7+5-10+mmol+meal+spacing) — 4–7 mmol/L before meals, 5–10 two hours after; eat every 4–6 hours and pair carbs with protein/fat
- [150 minutes and embrace inconvenience](https://www.google.com/search?q=diabetes+canada+150+minutes+physical+activity+sedentary) — weekly aerobic activity plus resistance training; mini movement breaks (microwave balance, pace on calls) reduce sedentary time
