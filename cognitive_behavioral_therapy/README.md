## Key Concepts

- [Anger Iceberg — primary vs secondary emotions](https://www.google.com/search?q=anger+iceberg+CBT+primary+secondary+emotions) — anger is often the visible tip masking hurt, shame, anxiety, grief, or even tiredness and hunger
- [4-4-6 breathing and the 30-minute diversion](https://www.google.com/search?q=CBT+anger+coping+deep+breathing+time+out) — reset physiology before reasoning; inhale 4s, pause 4s, exhale 6s, or divert for 30 minutes until arousal drops
- [Cognitive restructuring — Helicopter View](https://www.google.com/search?q=CBT+challenging+negative+thoughts+helicopter+view) — put irrational thoughts on trial with evidence checks, the friend test, and "will this matter in 1–5 years?"
- [Behavioral activation](https://www.google.com/search?q=behavioral+activation+CBT+depression+schedule) — schedule positive experiences with incremental goals when motivation is low
- [Mental health maintenance plan](https://www.google.com/search?q=mental+health+maintenance+plan+triggers+warning+signs+self+care) — manage triggers, warning signs, self-care habits, and pre-rehearsed coping strategies before crisis hits
