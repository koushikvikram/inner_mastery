## Sam Martin — Peak Performance

- [This Stops 99% of Athletes From Dominating Their Sport](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TYkXtE2xPoA) — Challenge vs threat appraisals: Ronaldo sees pressure as a challenge, not a threat; stress is an alarm to go forward, not run away; 3 tips: watch your language, self-coaching, reflection & roleplay
- [99% of Athletes Will Make These Mistakes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CxW6fCnufYg) — 7 mental mistakes: not playing the long game, waiting to feel perfect, expecting recognition, playing it too safe, constant obsession without recovery, not embracing the boring, suppressing emotions
- [How to Manage Your Emotions Like a Pro Athlete](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FzCn-mh5X_A) — 3 tools: meditation (mindfulness), emotional vocabulary (journaling 4-prompt technique), cognitive reappraisal (shift from threat to challenge)
- [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=47kLZbULW9M](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=47kLZbULW9M)

## Bill Beswick — Sports Psychologist

- [Ask Yourself These Three Questions — Lessons from Bill Beswick](https://medium.com/@thisis-Shitanshu/ask-yourself-these-three-questions-lessons-from-sports-psychologist-bill-beswick-a2139c2e8980) — Medium: What do you want? How badly do you want it? How much will you suffer?
- [Inside the Mind of a Winner — Train to Dominate](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Cw6xuzosn4s) — Mulligan Brothers (11 min): three levels of athlete — turns up, trains to compete, trains to dominate; fighter vs victim mentality; Parkinson's and the crossover to life
- [Bill Beswick — Full Interview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QOsHNL6V8O8) — Mulligan Brothers (75 min): confidence, belief, cohesion, handling pressure, athlete as a better person

## Daniel Goleman — Focus

- [Daniel Goleman on Focus: The Secret to High Performance and Fulfilment](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HTfYv3IEOqM) — Intelligence Squared (78 min): three kinds of focus (inner, other, outer); why attention is the hidden ingredient of excellence

## Books

- [The Inner Game of Tennis — Summary](https://www.readthechapters.com/the-inner-game-of-tennis--the-classic-guide-to-the-mental-side-of-peak-performance-summary) — W. Timothy Gallwey: Self 1 (the critical, overthinking mind) vs Self 2 (the natural, instinctive body); peak performance comes from quieting Self 1 and trusting Self 2

## Dandapani — Focus & Concentration

- [Unwavering Focus (TEDxReno)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4O2JK_94g3Y) — Awareness vs mind: awareness is a ball of light that travels the mind; concentration = keeping awareness on one thing; we become good at what we practice — most people practice distraction 16hrs/day
