# Epictetus Operating Model

## Layer 1 — Practice (What must be done?)

Focus on real-world behaviors, standards, and outcomes.

Questions:

* What are we trying to achieve?
* What actions must happen consistently?
* What does good execution look like?
* What habits or rules matter most?

Outputs:

* Routines
* Checklists
* Standards
* Deliverables
* Habits

---

## Layer 2 — Principles (Why does it work?)

Define the reasoning behind the practices.

Questions:

* Why do these actions matter?
* What principle are we protecting?
* What causes success or failure here?
* What tradeoffs are involved?

Outputs:

* Mental models
* Strategies
* Design principles
* Decision rules
* Doctrine

---

## Layer 3 — Meta (How do we evaluate and improve?)

Optimize, scale, measure, and refine the system.

Questions:

* How do we measure quality?
* How do we improve the process?
* What tools or systems help?
* How do we teach and scale this?

Outputs:

* Metrics
* Dashboards
* Automation
* Reviews
* Governance
* Training

---

## Core Rule

Never let Layer 3 replace Layer 1.

---

## Operating Sequence

1. Define Practice
2. Clarify Principles
3. Build Meta Systems

---

## Diagnostic Question

Are we failing at:

* Doing? → Layer 1
* Understanding? → Layer 2
* Improving? → Layer 3
