# Param Drṣṭvā Nivartate — The Principle of Higher Taste

> *"param dṛṣṭvā nivartate"* — "having experienced a higher taste, he is fixed in consciousness"
> — Bhagavad Gita 2.59

The idea that a lower pleasure is only truly abandoned when a higher experience replaces it — not by force of will, but by taste.
